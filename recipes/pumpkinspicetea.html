Pumpkin Spice Tea

Let's make some tea!

This black tea recipe is perfect for getting you in the fall mood. Follow the recipe below for a treat!

Ingredients

  • tea kettle
  • tea pot with steeper
  • hot water
  • black tea
  • milk of choice
  • cinnamon, nutmeg, cloves or mix of fall spices
  • (optional)whipped cream

Directions

  1. Bring water to a boil using tea kettle
  2. Follow tea instructions for black tea. Normally steep for 5 minutes
  3. Pour tea into a tea cup and milk into the tea until you've reached desired opacity
  4. Either top with fall spices or top with whipped cream and sprinkle fall spices on top of whipped cream
